Humanity Against Cards

Are you funnier than a computer?

* If you have not played before, when you enter this game you are given some cards and there are more people, each with their own cards.

* The server chooses a random sentence and players must choose one of their cards to complete the sentence and be as amusing as possible.

* One of the players, the "Judge", chosen on the turn, sits on the sidelines and votes the most amusing answer.

* The chosen card earns points. The game continues with another Judge, another sentence, etc. for a certain number of rounds.

* The winner is of course the one with the most points.
